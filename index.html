About Me

Hello! I'm Brian Odhiambo, a passionate software developer and engineer with a strong focus on technology and innovation. I specialize in Android app development and have gained significant experience in web development as well. My career is driven by a deep interest in creating impactful and user-centric technical solutions.

During my high school years, I channeled my passion into electronics, personally attempting and successfully repairing devices whenever they broke down. Although I initially aimed to enroll in Computer Science classes, I ended up in Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering. Despite this, my love for computing remains steadfast.

While at university, I conceived the idea for the Student Center Application, which has become one of my favorite projects. It reflects my dedication to integrating technology with practical needs, making it a significant milestone in my youthful journey.

In high school, I held leadership roles as the secretary for the disciplinary committee and the class secretary. Currently, I serve as the class secretary for the Mining Engineering class at the university. With the encouragement and support of my classmates, I am motivated to pursue higher positions within the department, and I am committed to striving for these opportunities.

In addition to my professional work, I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, coding, and playing music. My hobbies also include browsing the internet, watching TV, and walking. I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges that push the boundaries of technology.

Feel free to explore my projects and reach out if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work.
