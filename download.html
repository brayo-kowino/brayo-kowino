Student Center v5.16.29

App Description:

Student Center is a comprehensive and user-friendly application designed specifically for students. The app offers five main features: Notepad, Exam Analysis, Assignment Tracker, Internet Explorer, and Media Player, supporting both local and online media. While Exam Analysis is currently in its alpha phase, the other features are fully functional.

The Assignment Tracker helps manage assignments by their due dates, providing reminders to ensure timely submission. Notifications are sent on the due date at 8:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 5:00 PM, ensuring that users are reminded to submit their assignments.

Aware that students frequently use their smartphones, the Internet Explorer feature allows them to seamlessly browse the web when connected to the internet. The app aims to be a valuable tool for students, and future updates will introduce additional features.

While I strive for stability, some features may experience issues. Please report any problems you encounter, including details about the actions leading to the issue. I appreciate your feedback and will work to resolve these issues in future updates. Enjoy the various features of the app and help me improve Student Center for everyone!
